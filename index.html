To My Urvi ❤️

There were so many times you could have walked away… So many moments when I made it difficult to stay. In those first two years… I was immature. I was reactive. I let ego speak louder than love. I let anger speak louder than understanding. I hurt you without realizing how deeply words can cut. And yet… you stayed. You stayed when leaving would have been easier. You stayed when I was toxic, when I was stubborn, when I didn’t know how to love properly. And instead of giving up on me… you believed in the version of me I hadn’t even become yet. You didn’t just love me. You changed me. You taught me patience. You taught me softness. You taught me that love isn’t about winning arguments — it’s about protecting each other’s hearts. Every time you were about to leave but didn’t… every time you chose us over your pain… every time you gave me another chance when I barely deserved the first… you were building the man I am today. If I am calmer now… it’s because of you. If I think before I speak… it’s because of you. If I love deeper… it’s because you showed me how. You didn’t just become my girlfriend. You became my growth. My lesson. My mirror. My peace. And if I am your Spider-Man… thank you for being the perfect Gwen — the one who stays, who believes, who saves me in ways I never even realized I needed saving. I love you. Not just for who you are. But for who you helped me become. Forever yours.
